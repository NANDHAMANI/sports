Sport Center.

A sport center typically offers a variety of facilities and services focused on physical activity, fitness, and wellness. It may include:

Swimming Pool

For recreational swimming, fitness classes, and swimming lessons.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players each. The objective is to score points by shooting a ball through
the opponent's hoop, which is 10 feet high.

Track running

Track running is a sport that involves athletes racing on a standard oval-shaped track, typically in outdoor or indoor stadiums
